Analysis of Public Sentiment on Social Media Twitter Toward Gibran Rakabuming as a 2024 Vice Presidential Candidate.

In this project, I implemented the Sentiment Analysis Paper by Widayanti, C., & Fridiyanti, Y. N. (2023) into a stable-baselines3 implementation of PPO. Additionally, I reproduced the original sentiment analysis results by tracking my experiments using the SVM (Support Vector Machine) technique.

The code for this project can be found here.

I also wrote an article elaborating on the theory of SVM and my results and submitted it to Senovtik! You can find the article here.
